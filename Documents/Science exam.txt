Here's what each of those terms means:

Herbivores: Animals that only eat plants.

Examples: cows, deer, elephants, rabbits.

Carnivores: Animals that only eat other animals (meat).

Examples: lions, wolves, eagles, sharks.

Omnivores: Animals that eat both plants and animals.

Examples: humans, bears, pigs, crows.

These terms describe the diet or feeding habits of animals.
