About English Reading System

The English Reading System is designed to help learners of all ages improve their reading skills in English. Whether you are a beginner or looking to enhance your fluency, this system provides interactive stories, comprehension questions, and vocabulary practice to make reading enjoyable and effective.

  • Read short stories and passages tailored to your level.
  • Answer comprehension questions to test your understanding.
  • Learn new words and phrases with vocabulary exercises.
  • Track your progress and earn coins for correct answers!

Start your journey to better English reading today!
